My Journey Through Words

Discover the story behind the blog and the person behind the words

Hello, I'm Haymawon

Welcome to The Curious Haymawon! I’m a storyteller, dreamer, and a young soul who believes in the quiet magic of words — their power to connect, heal, and inspire.

My journey began in a small town, where I learned to appreciate life’s simple, often overlooked moments. After studying literature and philosophy, I spent several years exploring the depths of the human condition, collecting stories, emotions, and fleeting wonders that now shape my perspective.

This blog is my sanctuary - a place where I share thoughts on mindfulness, creativity, and finding joy in everyday moments. I write about the lessons I've learned, the beauty I've witnessed, and the small wonders that make life magical.

When I'm not writing, you'll find me sipping tea in a sunlit corner, tending to my small garden, or getting lost in a good book. I believe in slow living, deep connections, and the transformative power of kindness.

My Journey

2015-2017

Plot Twists & Potato Chips

Spent most of my time reading comic books and making questionable life choices. Young, dramatic, and powered by snacks.

2019-2021

Fading Out

The worst and best era. I found love, thought it was forever — but ended up broken instead. I started pulling away from people, keeping to myself more and more. Became distant, turned antisocial. That’s when I learned how to live quietly with what hurt and stopped expecting anyone to understand.

2022

Downfall

The downfall of me and myself.

2023

Empty Days

Lost interest in everything. Wasted time. Felt Useless, hopeless, helpless, dreamless. Just living worthless.

2024-Present

Current Studies

Studying tech, philosophy, the roots of words, literature, and languages. I enjoy exploring what deeply fascinates me — with curiosity, fun, and intention.
