Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

I try to respond to all messages within 24-48 hours. During weekends and holidays, it may take a little longer, but I promise to get back to you as soon as possible!

Do you accept guest posts or collaborations?

Yes, I'm always open to collaborations and guest posts! Please send your ideas to collab@haymawon.com with a brief outline of your concept. I review all submissions and will respond within a week.

Can I republish your content on my site?

I'm happy to share my content with proper attribution and a link back to the original post. Please email me with details about where you'd like to republish the content, and I'll get back to you with permission and guidelines.

How often do you post new content?

I publish new posts every Tuesday and Friday. Occasionally, I'll share bonus content on my social media channels. Subscribe to my newsletter to never miss an update!

Can I suggest topics for future posts?

Absolutely! I love hearing what my readers want to see. Feel free to email me your suggestions or mention them in the contact form. I read every suggestion and often incorporate them into my content calendar.
